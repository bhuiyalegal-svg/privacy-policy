This Privacy Policy explains how Bangladesh Ain & Salish Sohoyta Kendro - BASSK collects, uses, and protects your information when you interact with our Facebook Page, Messenger services, website, and online legal assistance platforms.
01

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Messages and legal inquiries submitted through Facebook Messenger
  • Case-related details voluntarily provided by users
  • Appointment and consultation information
  • Technical information such as Facebook User ID and interaction history
02

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide legal information and support services
  • Respond to Messenger inquiries and requests
  • Schedule consultations and legal assistance sessions
  • Improve our Facebook automation and communication systems
  • Maintain records for service quality and follow-up support
03

Facebook Messenger Integration

Bangladesh Ain & Salish Sohoyta Kendro - BASSK uses automated Facebook Messenger systems to communicate with users and provide support.

  • Automated replies may be used to answer common questions
  • Users may be asked to provide contact information for follow-up
  • Messenger data is securely stored for support purposes only
  • Our use of Messenger also follows Meta's Privacy Policy
We never sell user data or use private legal information for advertising purposes.
04

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. Data may only be shared:

  • With authorized members of our support or legal team
  • With trusted service providers used to operate our systems
  • When legally required by applicable law or court order
05

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for service purposes.

  • Messenger conversation data may be stored for up to 12 months
  • Support records may be retained for operational and legal purposes
  • You may request deletion of your information at any time
06

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Stop receiving Messenger communications
07

Cookies & Tracking

Our website or services may use cookies and analytics tools to improve user experience and service performance.

08

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published on this page with a revised update date.

10

Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us.
